Field methods used are described in Springs in the Mojave Desert Network—Surface water monitoring at desert springs: Standard operating procedures version 1.0, available here: https://irma.nps.gov/DataStore/Reference/Profile/2256063. Specific methods include SOP 4: Spring Acceptance and Classification, SOP 5: Water Availability: Flow Condition, SOP 6: Water Availability: Data-Logging Sensors, SOP 7: Water Quality, SOP 8: Site Condition: Spring Vegetation, SOP 9: Site Condition: Invasive Plants, SOP 10: Site Condition: Disturbance, and SOP 11: Site Condition: Repeat Photographs.
